Tutorial

Welcome to Sus Lumber Tycoon! Your goal is to run a lumber business for 10 weeks. You want to protect the environment and be Sus(tainable), so choose who you buy wood from wisely. You must also preserve and hopefully grow your business, so try your best to make a profit. Buy from suppliers and meet clients' quotas each week. Failure to do so will result in a fine. Fines double on week 8 and are multiplied by 6 on week 9. Each in-game week is 10 seconds, so think fast! You can press escape to exit the game. Good luck!
